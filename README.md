### Outlet Establishment Trend

### Fat Content Analysis
Regular fat products contribute significantly more revenue than low-fat products, indicating strong customer preference.

### Item Type Performance
Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the highest revenue-generating categories, while Seafood contributes the least.

Medium-sized outlets outperform small and high outlets in overall sales.

### Outlet Location Tier
Tier 3 locations generate the highest revenue compared to Tier 1 and Tier 2.

Supermarket Type1 dominates sales and item volume, making it the top-performing outlet type.
